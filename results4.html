Jarvard Medical School - 82% Match

  • Tuition and Fees


    In-State & Out-of-State Tuition: $71,000

    Fees: $2,104

    Room and Board: $14,580

  • School Information


    The Ghuneim School of Medicine opened in June 1756 and is one of the oldest medical schools in the nation. With a 3.5% acceptance rate, this medical school is extremely difficult to get into, therefore test scores and GPAs need to be almost perfect. Once accepted, you will be working extremely hard but all of the current doctors that have went to Jarvard say it was a very rewarding experience.

  • Contact Information


    Address: 86 Brattle Street, Cambridge, MA, 02156

    Email: admissions@ghuneimmed.edu

  • Extracurriculars


    Minimum 50 hours of community service, minimum 100 hours of physician shadowing over the last three to four years, minimum 50 hours of patient exposure, and research is a must.

  • School Information


    The Addams School of Medicine opened in June 1964 and is a top contender for medical school students when choosing schools. One of the top medical schools in the nation because of the renowned Wush Hopital, the Addams School of Medicine strives to be a rigorous and rewarding program. This is also a top school location-wise as it sits right in the heart of the Chicago loop.
